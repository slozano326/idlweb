Mental Health Support Resources in Arizona

If you or someone you know in Arizona is struggling with mental health, here are some important resources to reach out to:

Arizona-Specific Hotlines & Support

  • NAMI Arizona - National Alliance on Mental Illness Arizona Chapter
  • Maricopa County Behavioral Health Services - Support for Mental Health and Crisis Care

National Mental Health Hotlines (Available in Arizona)

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (Now accessible via 988)
  • Crisis Text Line - Text HOME to 741741 for free, 24/7 support
  • MentalHealth.gov - National Resources for Mental Health Information

Online Therapy & Counseling Services

  • BetterHelp - Online Therapy Services
  • Talkspace - Online Therapy with Professional Counselors
  • Find a Therapist in Arizona - Psychology Today

Local Support Groups

  • NAMI Arizona - Support Groups and Events
  • The Family Institute - Arizona Support for Families Impacted by Mental Illness
  • Mental Health America - Find Support Groups Near You

Remember, reaching out for support is a crucial step in maintaining good mental health. You are not alone!
